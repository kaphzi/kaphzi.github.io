Controls & notes:

  1. Click anywhere on the image to continue.

操作：

  1. 点击任何地方以继续。
  2. 按下空格/右箭头/E翻到下一张图、Q/左箭头返回上一张图。
  3. 按下Esc返回标题界面。

A/N:
I've always felt melancholic whenever I pass by the dreamcatchers in game. I think Charles would have wanted to hold on to those fading memories of his happiest times... This is also very lightly inspired by a fic I read where Charles cuts a piece of feather from a dreamcatcher, and that is where his epilogue feather came from. Also this whole project was the product of a single photo I did very early on of Arthur with herons in the background, which was originally just an echo of "blue heron leaves the northern sky" but it looked quite dreamy and paired with the dreamcatchers... (crying)
