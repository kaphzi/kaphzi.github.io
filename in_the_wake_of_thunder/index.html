A/N:
Henlo it's kaph :D I made this on a whim last year and spent a whole two months from October to Christmas making it. The ending hurt me a little too much so I thought I may as well make my own cutscenes, and so this is what I made. I wanted to create a convincing cutscene that spans the time from Charles going back to find Arthur alive, to their eventual happy ending. I tried to make it convincing in all aspects, from their developing relationship to where they stayed while Arthur healed (Silent Stead in New Austin), while keeping the image itself composed in a way that is cinematic and conveys feeling. Turns out the most difficult part was coming up with dialogue that both fit the image and helps convey the plot.

Anyway I posted this on Christmas and the responses was full of people bawling their eyes out so I guess I did a good job lol. I'm currently working on a continuation (Fool's Errand) so keep your eyes peeled for that!
