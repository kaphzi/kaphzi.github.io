A Stray Hat

A Stray Hat

掉落的帽子

Controls & notes:

  1. Click anywhere on the image to continue.
  2. When faced with choices, click on the dialogue option of your choosing.
  3. POV: Charles

操作：

  1. 点击任何地方以继续。
  2. 面临选择时，点击屏幕下方的对话选项。
  3. 查尔斯视角。

A/N:
This is an experimental project for experience using Twine and interactivity and branching. I'd like to make cutscenes with more branches and consequence of choice in the future.

I coded this very roughly with CSS/HTML to keep myself from getting rusty so the UX/UI are probably not the best; please bear with me.

The plot is directly inspired by a conversation in the QQ Charthur group chat. I'm making this with permission from @, who came up with this idea.

Constructive feedback appreciated. Please submit to tumblr ask box: @kaphzzz
