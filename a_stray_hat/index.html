A Stray Hat

掉落的帽子

Controls & notes:

  1. Click anywhere on the image to continue.
  2. When faced with choices, choose a dialogue option by clicking on it or pressing the corresponding key.
  3. POV: Charles

操作：

  1. 点击任何地方以继续。
  2. 面临选择时，点击或按下标明的按键来选择对话选项。
  3. 查尔斯视角。

A/N:
This is an experimental project for experience using Twine and interactivity and branching. I'd like to make cutscenes with more branches and consequence of choice in the future.



For a better viewing experience with a strictly linear sequence of images, I've disabled going backwards through the cutscene. Please restart to play through different options. The plot is directly inspired by a conversation in the QQ Charthur group chat. I'm making this with permission from @kuchidesha (Twitter), who came up with this idea! The main idea is about how without his hat, Arthur's an open book. His emotions and thoughts are written all over his face without the brim to hide underneath. Charles finds this immensely endearing, of course.

Constructive feedback appreciated. Please submit to tumblr ask box: @kaphzzz
